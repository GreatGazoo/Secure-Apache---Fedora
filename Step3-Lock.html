Step 3 - Locking down the SSL/TLS environment

Another option is to remove or add SSL/TLS Protocols - I highly recommend at a minimum to remove -SSLv2 and -SSLv3!!! To remove TLSv1 and TLSv1.1 support just add the lines -TLSv1 -TLSv1.1 +TLSv1.2. You can also use -all and +TLSv1.2. TLSv1.1 is rarely used and is not really that different from TLSv1. It is recommended, if you can at any chance, to use only TLSv1.2, but this change should be evaluated on its overall effect prior to making such a change. All of this will be discussed in the following sections.

Protocol Support

    TLSv1.1 -     <--- Do not use!!!
    TLSv1.2 -     <--- We will score 100% on all areas but Cipher Strength will be 90% To acheive 100 % on all tests, remove 128 bit cipher support.
